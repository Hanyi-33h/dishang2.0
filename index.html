公司简介

作为迪尚集团旗下高端时装制造板块的核心企业，大连迪尚华盛时装有限公司注册资本1.2亿元人民币，厂区占地约15000平方米，拥有12000平方米现代化生产车间，核心团队与一线员工共600余人，年产能40万件。

公司聚焦高端时装，尤其是双面呢大衣、风衣、西服及羽绒服等品类的精细化生产，以服装加工制造为核心，联动进出口贸易业务，构建了集研发、生产、贸易于一体的全流程服务体系，为全球客户提供高品质服装解决方案。

A core high-end fashion manufacturer under Dishang Group, Dalian Dishang Huasheng Fashion Co., Ltd. has RMB 120 million in registered capital. Our plant spans ~15,000 sqm, with 12,000 sqm of modern workshops, a team of over 600 employees, and an annual capacity of 400,000 pieces.

Specializing in high-end fashion — double-faced wool coats, trenchcoats, suits and down jackets — we integrate garment manufacturing with import/export trade. With a full R&D-production-trade service system, we deliver premium garment solutions to global clients.
